ABOT US

Keya Buildcon, is one of the leading Vadodara, Gujarat (India) based construction company builds Parks, Townships, Commercial Centers, Malls and Multiplexes in Gujarat.. We are committed for becoming best partner of construction industries. we provide flexible construction services that addresses the demands and needs of the Public and Private Sectors. Since our foundation, we have developed several projects based upon time to time requirement.

We have a National construction capability based on established Regional strengths that enables us to get close to our customers and our supply chain. Our network of regional offices covering whole of the Gujarat means we are an integral part of the communities where we work.

Mr. Anil Jiyani & Mr. Sureshbhai Satashiya, founder and chairmen of the company has done his Masters with Civil Engineering and has expertise in structure designing and planning having hugh working experiance of 15+ years in construction line. During these years, both have worked with many construction sites.

Vision : By keeping positive approach & ethics, supply excellent & quality constructions to customers as committed.

Mission : Company wants to keep continue holding its share in the growth of current infrastructure of the country.

Motto : We Build Trust.

Value : Simplicity, Affordability, Innovation, Quality, Commitments, Customer Satisfaction, Social Responsiveness
